Hey there! I'm Nadia

An Interactive Media Design student on a mission to improve user experience through beautiful and well thought out design.

Education

I am currently studying Interactive Media Design, in this program I am learning a variety of skills for digital media including graphic design, UX/UI, web design, design theory, and design best practices. In my program we learn through hands-on projects with sample clients where we create design assets for them including branding, graphics, and responsive web sites.

UX/UI Design

I have studied UX design through project-based courses. This has given me a holistic view of the UX design process. I have had the opportunity to work with a variety of sample clients and projects. I enjoy that UX design focuses on the user and their goals and that the solutions are based on real human research. I enjoy creating beautiful, functional redesigns and maximizing the potential of a product or service.

Responsive Web Design

I am learning responsive web design by creating a variety of sites that behave across a range of platforms. I use fluid design, media queries, CSS frame work and web animation to create fun, interactive and dynamic media projects.

BA Communications and Media Studies

In addition to learning Interactive Media Design, I have an Honours Degree in Communications and Media Studies. Through this degree I have practiced my communications and analytical skills. I have excellent written and spoken clear communication skills paired with the ability to apply a systemic approach to communications projects.
